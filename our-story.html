Our Story

A Journey of Heritage, Innovation, and Purity

Where It All Began

In the lush delta region of Konaseema, where the mighty Godavari River meets the Bay of Bengal, coconut trees have stood tall for over a century. This is where our story begins - not just as a business, but as a continuation of a rich agricultural heritage.

Our Journey Through Time

1900s

The Heritage Begins

Konaseema region establishes itself as a premier coconut cultivation area. Generations of farmers perfect the art of growing the finest coconuts in India.

2018

The Vision Takes Shape

Recognizing the untapped potential of Konaseema's coconut wealth, our founder envisioned bringing pure, organic virgin coconut oil to health-conscious consumers worldwide.

2019

Konaseema Agro Products is Born

We established our state-of-the-art cold-press facility, combining traditional wisdom with modern technology. Our first batch of virgin coconut oil was produced with zero heat, zero chemicals.

2020

Organic Certification Achieved

Received organic certification, validating our commitment to chemical-free, sustainable production. Launched hi-tech packaging facility ensuring leak-proof, hygienic packaging.

2021

Expanding Horizons

Entered B2B market, partnering with wholesalers, exporters, and manufacturers. Introduced bulk packaging options from 10L to tanker loads.

2022

Growing Trust

Crossed 5,000 happy customers milestone. Established partnerships with 200+ B2B clients across India. Began export operations to international markets.

2023

Innovation & Quality

Upgraded production capacity. Implemented advanced quality testing protocols. Launched custom packaging solutions for private label partners.

2024-25

Leading the Way

Serving 10,000+ retail customers and 500+ B2B partners. Recognized as a trusted supplier of premium virgin coconut oil. Continuing our mission of purity and sustainability.

A Message from Our Founder

"Growing up in Konaseema, I witnessed the incredible quality of coconuts our region produces. Yet, I saw how traditional oil extraction methods often compromised the natural goodness. I dreamed of creating a product that would honor both our heritage and modern health consciousness."

That dream became Konaseema Agro Products. We invested in cold-press technology that extracts oil without heat, preserving every nutrient, every benefit, every drop of purity that nature intended.

Today, when you choose our virgin coconut oil, you're not just buying a product - you're supporting sustainable farming, empowering local communities, and bringing centuries of Konaseema heritage into your home.

- Rajesh Kumar, Founder & CEO
