Discover the Konaseema Difference

Konaseema, a scenic delta region surrounded by the Godavari River and Bay of Bengal, has been renowned for its rich coconut cultivation heritage for over a century. Our virgin coconut oil is extracted through advanced cold-press technology from coconut milk, preserving every drop of nature's goodness.

Since 2019-2020, Konaseema Agro Products has been pioneering organic coconut oil production with hi-tech packing machines ensuring leak-proof, hygienic packaging that meets international standards.
