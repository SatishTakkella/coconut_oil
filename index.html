Discover the Konaseema Difference

Konaseema, a scenic delta region surrounded by the Godavari River and Bay of Bengal, has been renowned for its rich coconut cultivation heritage for over a century. Our virgin coconut oil is extracted through advanced cold-press technology from coconut milk, preserving every drop of nature's goodness.

Since 2019-2020, Konaseema Agro Products has been pioneering organic coconut oil production with hi-tech packing machines ensuring leak-proof, hygienic packaging that meets international standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our coconut oil

What makes Konaseema coconut oil special?

Konaseema region's unique soil, climate, and 100+ years of coconut cultivation heritage produce the finest coconuts. Our cold-pressed method within 24 hours preserves maximum nutrients and authentic flavor.

Is your coconut oil really chemical-free?

Absolutely! We use traditional wooden press cold-pressing method without any heat, chemicals, or preservatives. Every batch is lab-tested for purity.

How should I store the coconut oil?
