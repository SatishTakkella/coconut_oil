The Konaseema Difference

Nestled in the fertile delta of East Godavari, Konaseema is renowned as the coconut paradise of Andhra Pradesh. The unique soil composition, abundant water resources, and tropical climate create the perfect conditions for growing the finest coconuts in India.

Our coconuts are harvested from traditional groves that have been nurtured for generations. Each coconut is hand-selected at peak ripeness, ensuring maximum nutritional value and authentic flavor.

The cold-pressing process begins within 24 hours of harvest, preserving the natural goodness, aroma, and health benefits that make Konaseema coconut oil truly exceptional.
