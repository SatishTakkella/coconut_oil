Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

MOQ varies by packaging: 50L for 10L packs, 200L for jerrycans, 1000L for drums, and 12MT for tankers. Custom orders are negotiable.

Do you provide samples?

Yes, we provide samples for quality testing. Sample charges are refundable on bulk orders above 500L.

What are the payment terms?

We offer flexible payment terms including advance payment, LC, and credit terms for established clients. Terms vary based on order size.

How long does delivery take?

Typically 7-15 days depending on location and order size. Express delivery available for urgent requirements.

Do you export internationally?

Yes, we export to multiple countries with complete documentation, certifications, and export-ready packaging.

Can you provide private labeling?

Yes, we offer private label and custom packaging solutions. MOQ and pricing depend on specifications.
