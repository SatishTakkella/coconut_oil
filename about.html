About Konaseema Agro Products

Preserving Heritage, Delivering Purity

Who We Are

Konaseema Agro Products is a pioneering organic coconut oil manufacturer based in the heart of East Godavari, Andhra Pradesh. Since 2019-2020, we've been committed to producing the finest quality virgin coconut oil using traditional wisdom and modern technology.

Our name comes from Konaseema, a scenic delta region surrounded by the Godavari River and Bay of Bengal, renowned for over 100 years of rich coconut cultivation heritage. This fertile land produces some of the finest coconuts in India, and we're proud to transform them into premium virgin coconut oil.

We combine advanced cold-press extraction technology with hi-tech packing machines to ensure every bottle meets international quality standards while preserving the natural goodness of coconuts.
