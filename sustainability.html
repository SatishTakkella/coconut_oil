Empowering Communities

Supporting Local Farmers

We work directly with over 100 farmer families in the Konaseema region, providing:

  • Fair and stable pricing for their coconuts
  • Training in organic farming techniques
  • Access to organic certification programs
  • Financial support during off-seasons
  • Healthcare and education assistance for farming families
  • Women's empowerment through skill development programs
